▶ Essential Tips
  • Kids under 11 travel FREE on Tube/bus with a paying adult
  • Family Railcard (£30) saves 1/3 on trains and 60% off kids' fares
  • Free museums: Natural History, Science, British Museum, V&A
  • June: Sunrise ~4:45, sunset ~21:20. Average 15-22°C
  • Avoid rush hours with kids (8-9:30, 17-18:30)
