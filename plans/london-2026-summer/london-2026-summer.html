Getting Around

Within London

  • Tube (Underground) — Fastest way around. Use contactless bank card or Oyster card. £2.80/ride (Zone 1), daily cap ~£8.10. Kids under 11 ride FREE with a paying adult
  • Bus — £1.75/ride, daily cap £5.25. Hop-on hop-off with contactless. Great for sightseeing from the top deck. Bus 11 (King's Road to Liverpool Street) is a classic scenic route
  • Thames Clippers (Uber Boat) — River bus service. ~£8-10/trip, accepts Oyster/contactless. Fun alternative for Greenwich, Westminster, Canary Wharf routes
  • Walking — Central London is very walkable. Most attractions within 15-20 min walk of each other. Expect 8-12 miles/day

Day Trips from London

  • Windsor — GWR train from Paddington, ~30 min, £12-15 return. Direct service every 30 min
  • Cotswolds — Car hire recommended (~£50-70/day). Alternatively, guided tour from London (~£60-80/ad). No practical direct train to the villages
  • Family & Friends Railcard (£30/year) — saves 1/3 off adult fares and 60% off children's fares. Pays for itself on the Windsor trip alone

▶ London Eye
~30 min £30/ad, £25/ch South Bank, Waterloo
The London Eye is a 135-metre tall observation wheel on the South Bank of the River Thames, offering breathtaking 360-degree views of the city skyline. On a clear day you can see up to 40 kilometres, spotting landmarks like Big Ben, The Shard, St Paul's Cathedral, and Buckingham Palace. Each of the 32 sealed glass capsules holds up to 25 passengers and completes one full rotation in approximately 30 minutes.
For families, the London Eye is an ideal experience — the capsules are spacious enough for strollers, and young children are mesmerised by the slowly changing panorama. June evenings offer particularly golden light for photos. The experience is wheelchair and buggy accessible, with no steps involved. Consider booking a late afternoon slot for the best light and slightly smaller crowds.
伦敦眼是一座高135米的观景摩天轮，矗立在泰晤士河南岸，可以360度俯瞰整个城市天际线。天气晴好时，视野可达40公里，大本钟、碎片大厦、圣保罗大教堂和白金汉宫等地标尽收眼底。摩天轮共有32个密封玻璃座舱，每个可容纳25名乘客，完成一圈大约需要30分钟。
对于家庭游客来说，伦敦眼是非常理想的体验——座舱空间宽敞，可以容纳婴儿车，小朋友们会被缓缓变化的全景深深吸引。六月的傍晚光线格外金黄，非常适合拍照。整个体验无障碍通行，没有台阶。建议预订下午较晚的时段，光线最佳且人流稍少。
Tickets: Book timed slots at londoneye.com. Standard: £30/adult, £25/child. Online booking is 10-20% cheaper than walk-up. Family tickets available.
▶ SEA LIFE London Aquarium
~1.5-2 hours £28/ad, £23/ch South Bank, next to London Eye
Located in the grand County Hall building right next to the London Eye, SEA LIFE London Aquarium is home to over 600 species of marine life across 14 themed zones. Children can walk through an underwater glass tunnel surrounded by sharks and rays, meet Gentoo penguins, touch rock pool creatures in the interactive zone, and explore a recreated rainforest environment.
The aquarium is especially well-suited for younger children (ages 3-10), with interactive displays at kid-friendly heights and regular feeding demonstrations throughout the day. Allow 1.5-2 hours for a comfortable visit without rushing. The gift shop at the exit is large and tempting — set expectations with kids beforehand!
伦敦海洋生物水族馆位于伦敦眼旁边宏伟的郡政厅大楼内，拥有14个主题区域中超过600种海洋生物。孩子们可以穿过被鲨鱼和鳐鱼环绕的水下玻璃隧道，与巴布亚企鹅见面，在互动区触摸潮池生物，还可以探索仿真的热带雨林环境。
水族馆特别适合低龄儿童（3-10岁），互动展示设置在适合儿童的高度，全天定期进行喂食表演。建议预留1.5-2小时从容参观。出口处的纪念品商店很大且充满诱惑——建议提前和孩子们做好心理准备！
Tickets: Book at visitsealife.com. Up to 20% off online vs walk-up. Combo tickets with London Eye available for additional savings.
▶ South Bank Walk
~1 hour Free Waterloo to Blackfriars
The South Bank is London's vibrant riverside promenade stretching from Westminster Bridge to Blackfriars Bridge. This pedestrian-friendly walkway buzzes with life — street performers, living statues, and musicians entertain crowds while the second-hand book market under Waterloo Bridge offers treasures for browsers. The skateboard park under the brutalist Southbank Centre is mesmerising to watch.
For families, the South Bank offers free entertainment that keeps kids engaged. Gabriel's Wharf has a riverside playground perfect for burning off energy, and Jubilee Gardens provides open green space with stunning views of Big Ben across the river. On warm June evenings, this stretch comes alive with pop-up food stalls, outdoor cinema screenings, and festival events.
南岸是伦敦充满活力的河畔步行道，从威斯敏斯特桥一直延伸到黑衣修士桥。这条步行友好的走道热闹非凡——街头艺人、活雕像和音乐家为人群带来欢乐，滑铁卢桥下的二手书市场则为淘书爱好者提供了寻宝之地。南岸中心粗犷主义建筑下的滑板公园也非常值得驻足观看。
对于家庭游客，南岸提供了丰富的免费娱乐活动。Gabriel's Wharf有一个河畔游乐场，非常适合孩子们释放精力；朱比利花园则提供开阔的绿地空间，可以欣赏对岸大本钟的壮丽景色。在温暖的六月傍晚，这一带会变得更加热闹，有临时美食摊位、露天电影放映和各类节日活动。
▶ Buckingham Palace & Changing of the Guard
~1.5 hours Free St James's Park, Green Park Tube
Buckingham Palace has served as the official London residence of the British monarch since 1837. The Changing of the Guard ceremony is one of London's most iconic free spectacles — soldiers in scarlet tunics and bearskin hats march to military band music in a precisely choreographed handover of duties. In June, the ceremony typically takes place daily at 11:00am and lasts about 45 minutes.
Arrive at least 30 minutes early to secure a good viewing spot along the palace gates. The Victoria Memorial in front of the palace offers elevated positions. For kids, the spectacle of mounted cavalry, gleaming swords, and military music is genuinely exciting. Check the schedule at changing-guard.com as it can be cancelled in heavy rain.
白金汉宫自1837年起一直是英国君主在伦敦的官方住所。卫兵换岗仪式是伦敦最具标志性的免费观赏活动之一——身穿猩红色制服、头戴熊皮帽的士兵在军乐队伴奏下，进行精心编排的换岗交接。六月期间，换岗仪式通常每天上午11:00举行，持续约45分钟。
建议至少提前30分钟到达，以便在宫门旁占据好的观赏位置。宫前的维多利亚纪念碑可以提供较高的视角。对于孩子们来说，骑兵、闪亮的佩剑和军乐的壮观场面真的非常令人兴奋。请在 changing-guard.com 查看时间表，大雨天可能会取消。
▶ St James's Park
~45 min Free Between Buckingham Palace & Westminster
St James's Park is the oldest of London's eight Royal Parks, originally a marshy watermeadow drained by Henry VIII for deer hunting. Today it is an immaculately maintained 23-hectare oasis with a central lake, ornamental flowerbeds, and ancient plane trees. The park's resident pelicans — descendants of birds gifted by the Russian Ambassador in 1664 — are fed daily at 2:30pm near Duck Island.
The views from the Blue Bridge spanning the lake are among London's finest — looking east toward the fairy-tale turrets of Horse Guards, and west toward Buckingham Palace framed by weeping willows. In June, the flowerbeds are at their most spectacular. The park's playground near the Birdcage Walk entrance is well-equipped for young children.
圣詹姆斯公园是伦敦八大皇家公园中历史最悠久的一个，最初是亨利八世为猎鹿而排干的沼泽地。如今，它是一片精心维护的23公顷绿洲，拥有中央湖泊、观赏花坛和古老的悬铃木。公园里常驻的鹈鹕——1664年俄罗斯大使赠送的鸟类后代——每天下午2:30在鸭岛附近接受喂食。
从横跨湖面的蓝桥上看到的景色堪称伦敦最美之一——向东望去是骑兵卫队大楼童话般的塔楼，向西则是垂柳掩映中的白金汉宫。六月的花坛最为壮观。公园在鸟笼步道入口附近的游乐场设施齐全，非常适合年幼的孩子。
▶ Westminster Abbey
~1.5 hours £27/ad, 6-17 £12, under 6 free Westminster Tube
Westminster Abbey is one of the world's great churches — the coronation church of the British monarchy since 1066 and the final resting place of 17 monarchs, along with celebrated figures from Charles Darwin to Charles Dickens. The Gothic architecture soars to dizzying heights, with fan vaulting, medieval tombs, and the famous Poets' Corner where literary giants are memorialised.
For families, the Abbey offers a multimedia guide with a special children's trail that turns the visit into a treasure hunt through history. The Chapter House features some of the finest medieval floor tiles in England. Allow about 1.5 hours; the cloisters and College Garden (oldest cultivated garden in England, free with admission) provide a peaceful escape from the crowds inside.
威斯敏斯特大教堂是世界上最伟大的教堂之一——自1066年以来一直是英国君主的加冕教堂，也是17位君主以及达尔文、狄更斯等名人的安息之所。哥特式建筑高耸入云，拥有扇形拱顶、中世纪墓葬和著名的诗人角，众多文学巨匠在此被纪念。
对于家庭游客，大教堂提供多媒体导览和专门的儿童探索路线，将参观变成一场穿越历史的寻宝之旅。议事厅拥有英格兰最精美的中世纪地砖。建议预留约1.5小时；回廊和学院花园（英格兰最古老的人工花园，凭门票免费参观）提供了远离人群的宁静空间。
Tickets: Book at westminster-abbey.org. £27/adult, children 6-17 £12, under 6 free. Multimedia guide included.
▶ Hyde Park & Diana Memorial Playground
~1-2 hours Free Lancaster Gate / Queensway Tube
Hyde Park and the adjoining Kensington Gardens form a 253-hectare green expanse in the heart of London. The park has been a royal hunting ground, a venue for the Great Exhibition of 1851, and a site of free speech at Speakers' Corner. The Serpentine lake offers pedal boats in summer, and the Rose Garden is magnificent in June bloom.
The undisputed highlight for families is the Diana Memorial Playground in Kensington Gardens — a massive wooden pirate ship surrounded by teepees, sensory trails, and musical instruments, all inspired by Peter Pan. It's regularly voted one of London's best playgrounds. Free entry, open daily. A second smaller playground exists near the Serpentine. On warm June evenings, the park is perfect for a sunset picnic.
海德公园与毗邻的肯辛顿花园共同构成了伦敦市中心253公顷的广阔绿地。这座公园曾是皇家猎场、1851年万国博览会的举办地，也是演讲者之角的自由言论场所。蛇形湖在夏季提供脚踏船，六月盛开的玫瑰园美不胜收。
对家庭来说，最大的亮点是肯辛顿花园内的戴安娜纪念游乐场——一艘巨大的木制海盗船，周围环绕着帐篷、感官探索小径和乐器，全部以彼得·潘为灵感设计。它经常被评为伦敦最佳游乐场之一，免费入场，每天开放。蛇形湖附近还有一个较小的游乐场。温暖的六月傍晚，公园非常适合来一场日落野餐。
▶ Natural History Museum
~2-3 hours Free South Kensington Tube
The Natural History Museum is housed in a magnificent Romanesque cathedral-like building designed by Alfred Waterhouse in 1881. The vast Hintze Hall greets visitors with the skeleton of a blue whale suspended from the ceiling — at 25 metres long, it is one of the most awe-inspiring museum entrances in the world. The collection holds over 80 million specimens spanning 4.5 billion years of life on Earth.
For children aged 5-9, the Dinosaur Gallery is an absolute must — featuring animatronic T-Rex, genuine fossils, and interactive dig pits. The earthquake simulator in the Earth galleries lets you experience the 1995 Kobe earthquake. In June, the Wildlife Garden is open and buzzing with real butterflies and insects. Arrive right at opening (10:00am) to explore the dinosaur galleries before the midday crowds descend.
自然历史博物馆坐落在阿尔弗雷德·沃特豪斯于1881年设计的宏伟罗马式建筑中。宽敞的欣策大厅迎接访客的是悬挂在天花板上的蓝鲸骨架——长25米，是世界上最令人叹为观止的博物馆入口之一。馆藏超过8000万件标本，跨越45亿年的地球生命史。

▶ Science Museum
~2-3 hours Free; Wonderlab £10/pp South Kensington Tube
The Science Museum brings science to life through hands-on exhibits spanning space exploration, computing history, engineering, and medicine. Highlights include a replica of Stephenson's Rocket, an Apollo 10 command module, and the earliest computers. The museum's approach is firmly interactive — designed to inspire rather than lecture.

▶ Tower of London
~3+ hours £33/ad, £16/ch Tower Hill Tube
The Tower of London is a 950-year-old fortress that has served as a royal palace, prison, armoury, and treasury. Founded by William the Conqueror in 1066, it houses the Crown Jewels — a dazzling collection of crowns, sceptres, and orbs still used in coronation ceremonies. The White Tower, the oldest part, contains a remarkable collection of royal armour including suits made for Henry VIII.
Join a free Yeoman Warder (Beefeater) tour for entertaining tales of executions, escapes, and royal intrigue — tours depart every 30 minutes from the main entrance and are excellent for families. Children receive free activity trails at the ticket desk. Look for the Tower's famous ravens — legend says if they ever leave, the kingdom will fall. Arrive at opening (9:00am) and head straight to the Crown Jewels to avoid the longest queues.
伦敦塔是一座有950年历史的堡垒，曾先后用作皇家宫殿、监狱、军械库和国库。它由征服者威廉于1066年建造，收藏着皇冠珠宝——一系列至今仍在加冕典礼上使用的璀璨王冠、权杖和宝球。最古老的白塔中收藏了精美的皇家盔甲，包括为亨利八世制作的铠甲套装。

▶ Tower Bridge Exhibition
~45 min £12/ad, £6/ch 5 min from Tower of London
Tower Bridge is London's most recognisable bridge, a Victorian engineering marvel that has spanned the Thames since 1894. The Exhibition takes you inside the bridge's twin towers and along the high-level glass walkways 42 metres above the river. The glass floor sections let you look straight down at the traffic and river below — a thrilling experience for brave kids!
The Victorian Engine Rooms beneath the bridge reveal the original steam-powered hydraulic machinery that once raised the bridge's bascules. Check the bridge lift schedule online — if you time it right, you can watch the bridge open for tall ships from the walkway above, a spectacular sight. Walking across the bridge itself is always free and offers excellent photo opportunities.
塔桥是伦敦最具辨识度的桥梁，一座自1894年横跨泰晤士河的维多利亚时代工程奇迹。展览带你进入桥的双塔内部，沿着河面上方42米的高空玻璃走道行走。玻璃地板部分可以让你直接俯瞰下方的车辆和河水——对勇敢的孩子来说是一次刺激的体验！
桥下的维多利亚时代机房展示了曾经升起桥面的原始蒸汽液压机械。可以在网上查看桥面升起的时间表——如果时间恰好，你可以从上方走道观看大桥为高桅船只开启的壮观景象。步行过桥本身始终免费，也是极好的拍照机会。
Tickets: Book at towerbridge.org.uk. £12.30/adult, £5.70/child.
▶ HMS Belfast
~1.5-2 hours £26/ad, under 16 free London Bridge area
HMS Belfast is a WWII light cruiser permanently moored on the Thames near Tower Bridge. Launched in 1938, she played key roles in the Battle of North Cape and the D-Day landings. Visitors can explore nine decks of the ship, from the captain's bridge to the boiler and engine rooms deep in the hull, experiencing what life was like for the crew of 950 men.
Children love scrambling through the narrow passageways, sitting in the gun turrets, and exploring the cramped mess decks where sailors ate and slept. The interactive exhibits include a simulated firing experience in the operations room. Under-16s enter free with a paying adult, making it excellent family value. The ship is not fully wheelchair/buggy accessible due to steep ladders between decks.
贝尔法斯特号是一艘二战轻巡洋舰，永久停泊在塔桥附近的泰晤士河上。1938年下水，她在北角海战和诺曼底登陆中发挥了关键作用。游客可以探索舰船的九层甲板，从舰长室到船体深处的锅炉和机房，体验950名船员的生活。
孩子们喜欢在狭窄的通道中穿梭、坐在炮塔里、探索水手们吃饭和睡觉的拥挤餐厅甲板。互动展品包括作战室中的模拟射击体验。16岁以下儿童在付费成人陪同下免费入场，性价比很高。由于甲板之间有陡峭的梯子，该舰不完全适合轮椅/婴儿车通行。
Tickets: Book at iwm.org.uk. £26/adult, under 16 free. Annual pass option available.
▶ Windsor Castle
~3 hours £30/ad, £16.50/ch Windsor, Berkshire (30 min train)
Windsor Castle is the oldest and largest occupied castle in the world, and the King's preferred weekend residence. Founded by William the Conqueror in the 11th century, the castle has been continuously occupied for over 900 years. The State Apartments are lavishly decorated with paintings by Rembrandt, Rubens, and Canaletto, while St George's Chapel is a masterpiece of Gothic architecture and the burial place of 10 monarchs.
The undeniable highlight for children is Queen Mary's Dolls' House — the most famous dolls' house in the world, designed by Sir Edwin Lutyens in the 1920s. Every detail is exquisitely crafted at 1:12 scale, including working electricity, running water, and a wine cellar with real vintage wines in miniature bottles. The audio guide (included) has a specific children's version. Allow a full morning for a thorough visit.
温莎城堡是世界上最古老、最大的有人居住的城堡，也是国王首选的周末居所。由征服者威廉于11世纪创建，城堡已被连续使用超过900年。国事厅以伦勃朗、鲁本斯和卡纳莱托的画作奢华装饰，而圣乔治教堂则是哥特式建筑的杰作，也是10位君主的安葬之地。
对孩子们来说，最大的亮点是玛丽王后的玩偶屋——世界上最著名的玩偶屋，由埃德温·鲁琴斯爵士于1920年代设计。每个细节都按1:12比例精心制作，包括可工作的电力系统、自来水，甚至还有装有真正微缩版陈年葡萄酒的酒窖。语音导览（包含在门票中）有专门的儿童版本。建议预留一整个上午进行充分参观。
Tickets: Book at rct.uk. £30/adult, £16.50/child (5-17), under 5 free. Audio guide included. Ask for a "Concessionary Admission" receipt at exit to convert your ticket to a 1-year pass at no extra cost.
▶ British Museum
~2-3 hours Free Holborn / Tottenham Court Road Tube
The British Museum holds a collection of over 8 million objects spanning two million years of human history. It is the most visited attraction in the UK, and its treasures include the Rosetta Stone (the key to deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphics), the Parthenon Sculptures, Egyptian mummies, Sutton Hoo Anglo-Saxon treasures, and Samurai armour from feudal Japan. The Great Court, with its spectacular glass roof designed by Norman Foster, is one of the largest covered public squares in Europe.
For families with young children, pick up a free family trail and activity backpack at the information desk — these turn the visit into an interactive adventure with drawing, searching, and discovery challenges. The Egyptian galleries (Rooms 62-63) with real mummies are the biggest hit with kids aged 5-9. Don't try to see everything; focus on 3-4 galleries over 2 hours. The museum cafe in the Great Court is a pleasant lunch spot.
大英博物馆收藏了超过800万件物品，跨越200万年的人类历史。它是英国参观人数最多的景点，珍品包括罗塞塔石碑（破译埃及象形文字的关键）、帕特农神庙雕塑、埃及木乃伊、萨顿胡盎格鲁-撒克逊宝藏和日本封建时代的武士盔甲。由诺曼·福斯特设计的大中庭拥有壮观的玻璃穹顶，是欧洲最大的室内公共广场之一。
对于带小孩的家庭，可以在咨询台领取免费的家庭探索路线图和活动背包——这些将参观变成一场包含绘画、搜索和发现挑战的互动冒险。埃及馆（62-63号展厅）的真实木乃伊最受5-9岁孩子欢迎。不要试图看完所有展品，建议在2小时内专注于3-4个展厅。大中庭的咖啡馆是一个不错的午餐地点。

▶ Covent Garden
~1 hour Free Covent Garden Tube
Covent Garden's covered market piazza, designed by Inigo Jones in the 1630s, is one of London's most atmospheric public spaces. The elegant iron-and-glass market building, dating from 1830, now houses boutique shops, craft stalls, and cafes. But the real draw is the street performance programme — licensed performers stage magic shows, acrobatics, opera, and comedy acts throughout the day in the North Piazza.
Children are captivated by the street performers, particularly the magic acts and acrobats who often invite young audience members to participate. 伦敦交通博物馆就位于考文特花园广场上，通过保存完好的交通工具追溯了城市200年的交通历史。孩子们可以登上一辆真正的维多利亚时代马拉公共汽车，坐在真实地铁列车的驾驶室里，操作互动信号杆。馆藏包括标志性的红色Routemaster巴士、早期地铁车厢和哈里·贝克设计的原版地铁线路图。

▶ Camden Market
~1.5 hours Free to browse Camden Town Tube
Camden Market is actually a collection of interconnected markets stretching along Camden High Street and the Regent's Canal. With over 1,000 stalls, it's a wonderfully chaotic mix of vintage clothing, handmade crafts, quirky gifts, and some of London's best street food. ▶ Bourton-on-the-Water

Known as the "Venice of the Cotswolds," Bourton-on-the-Water is arguably the prettiest village in England. ▶ Cutty Sark
~1-1.5 hours £16/ad, £8/ch Greenwich, Cutty Sark DLR
The Cutty Sark is the world's sole surviving tea clipper and the fastest ship of her era, built in 1869 to race tea from China to London. Now magnificently restored and raised on a glass support structure in Greenwich, visitors can walk beneath the gleaming copper hull — an extraordinary engineering experience — as well as explore the decks above where the crew lived and worked.
Children are fascinated by the below-deck living quarters, the enormous ship's wheel they can turn, and the cargo hold stories of tea-laden voyages around the world. Interactive digital displays throughout the ship bring maritime history to life. The glass-enclosed dry dock beneath the ship creates a unique space where you can touch the original hull planking. 卡蒂萨克号是世界上唯一幸存的茶叶快速帆船，也是其时代最快的船只，建于1869年，专为从中国运茶到伦敦的竞速而造。如今它在格林尼治被精心修复，架设在玻璃支撑结构上。游客可以在闪亮的铜质船底下方行走——这是一次非凡的工程体验——也可以探索船员生活和工作的上层甲板。

▶ National Maritime Museum

The National Maritime Museum is the world's largest maritime museum, housed in a stunning colonnaded building in Greenwich. ▶ Royal Observatory Greenwich
~1-1.5 hours £18/ad, £9/ch Greenwich Park (15 min uphill from Maritime Museum)
The Royal Observatory is where time and space begin — literally. It sits on the Prime Meridian Line at 0° longitude, the reference point for Greenwich Mean Time and every time zone on Earth. Founded in 1675 by Charles II, it was here that astronomers solved the problem of determining longitude at sea, revolutionising global navigation. The red Time Ball on the roof has dropped at exactly 1:00pm every day since 1833.
The famous Meridian Line is marked by a steel strip in the courtyard — children love straddling the line with one foot in the Eastern hemisphere and one in the Western. Inside, the Flamsteed House contains beautiful historic telescopes and clocks. The Peter Harrison Planetarium offers stunning digital shows about the night sky (separate ticket, very much worth it for kids). The walk up through Greenwich Park rewards you with one of London's finest panoramic views.

Practical Tips

Money

  • Contactless payment accepted almost everywhere. Apple Pay / Google Pay widely used
  • Carry ~£30-50 cash for small market stalls and street food vendors
  • ATMs (cash machines) are free at major banks. Avoid standalone ATMs with fees

June Weather & Daylight

  • Daylight: Sunrise ~4:45, sunset ~21:20 — incredibly long days for sightseeing
  • Temperature: Average 15-22°C, can reach 28°C+. Pack layers + light rain jacket
  • London rain is usually light drizzle, not downpours. A packable umbrella is essential

Travelling with Kids (5-9)

  • Kids under 11 travel FREE on Tube and bus with a paying adult
  • Avoid Tube rush hours (8-9:30am, 5-6:30pm) — trains are packed and stressful with kids
  • Carry snacks and water always. London is a walking city (expect 8-12 miles/day)
  • Schedule rest time after lunch. Days 3 and 7 have built-in lighter afternoons
  • Rainy day backups: Science Museum, Natural History Museum, Hamleys toy store
  • Check TfL step-free access if using a buggy/stroller — many Tube stations have stairs
